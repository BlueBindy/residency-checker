To be eligible, you need to have accrued sufficient days in the Republic of Ireland within the last 9 years. Any hours present in Ireland during a day count, so include departure and arrival days as present. Full days in Northern Ireland, or elsewhere in the UK do NOT count as present in the Republic of Ireland.
