Irish Residency Checker

Are you a British citizen looking to apply for Irish naturalisation through residency? You can check your residency period here to see if you qualify yet.

To be eligible, you need to have accrued the equivalent of five years of residency (1826 days less allowable absences of 42 days per year) in the Republic of Ireland within the last nine years (3,287 days). Any hours present during a day in Ireland allow you to count that day, so include departure and arrival days of travel. Full days in Northern Ireland, or elsewhere in the UK, or in any other country cannot be included. Note that leap years (eg 2016, 2020, 2024, 2028) have 366 days.

In addition to total residential days, you will also need to meet the final year 'continuous residency' requirement (323 days plus). You can check that below as well. Scroll down for a summary of your eligibility.
