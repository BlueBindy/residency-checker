Irish Residency Checker

Are you a British citizen looking to apply for Irish naturalisation through residency? You can check your residency period here to see if you qualify yet.

To be eligible, you need to have accrued sufficient days of residency in the Republic of Ireland within the last 3,287 days (9 years). Any hours present during a day in Ireland allow you to count that day, so include departure and arrival days. Full days in Northern Ireland, or elsewhere in the UK, or in any other country cannot be included.

In addition to total residential days, you will also need to meet the final year 'continuous residency' requirement. You can check that below as well. Scroll down for a summary of your eligibility.
